Overview

Chess is a popular 2 player strategy board game where the goal of each player is to capture the opponent's king. The game is played in a board with a 8 x 8 grid with 64 squares. Each player begins the game with 16 peices, 8 pawns, 2 bishops, rooks, knights, 1 queen, and 1 king. The game is played international and many tournaments are host for the game. The game can be played online with people across the world. Some organizations recognizes chess as a sport, but some may argue that chess is not a sport. Recently, computers have been designed to play chess at a professional level, and even more recently, had successfully defeated grandmasters, a person who has mastered the game of chess.
